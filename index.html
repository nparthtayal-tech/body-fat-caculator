How It Works

This calculator uses the U.S. Navy Body Fat Calculation Method, which is based on body circumference measurements (neck, waist, and hip for women).

Measurements Tips:

  • Measure at the smallest part of the neck, just below the larynx
  • Measure waist at your navel or smallest circumference
  • For women, measure hip at the largest point of buttocks
  • Keep measurements snug but not tight
